Requirements

Here we list both our hardware and software requirements for our device.
We listed our requirements using the MoSCoW method.

First we will look at our hardware requirements:

Req ID Description MoSCoW
1 The device must receive input from a physical 12.5mm keyboard Must Have
2 The device must receive input through a capacitive multi-touch touch screen Must Have
3 The device must have a colour screen - 720 x 1280 pixels at 270 ppi Must Have
4 The device must receive input through the use of stylus Must Have
5 The device must output sound through a headphone jack Must Have
6 The device must have a battery life of 10-20 hours Must Have
7 The device must have Wifi Connectivity Must Have
8 The device must have Bluetooth Connectivity Must Have
9 The device must have an ARM processor Must Have
10 The device must have at least 1Gb of internal RAM Must Have
11 The device should output sound through a speaker Should Have
12 The device shall have at least 2Gb of internal secondary storage Should Have
13 The device shall have a clamshell design Should Have
14 The device shall have expandable memory through a microsd card slot Should Have
15 The device shall have a camera Should Have
16 The device shall have a microphone Should Have
17 The device could have a hinged design, allowing for a tablet form and clamshell form Could Have


Next we will look at our software requirements:

Req ID Description MoSCoW
1 The device will run on the Android platform Must Have
2 The device must allow the user to have direct access to key apps (Here direct access to meant to mean 1 / 2 clicks) Must Have
3 The device must allow Android applications to be installed Must Have
4 The device must allow access to all other installed applications Must Have
5 The device must include a file explorer Must Have
6 The device shall include a custom applications explorer Should Have
7 The device shall have preloaded Psion applications Should Have
8 The device shall allow the user to customise their key apps Should Have
